Our Events

RAZZLE DAZZLE

This event is packed with full fun and based on creative ideas related to mathematics.

RAZZLE DAZZLE

Event Type: Single

Round 1: Hot potato

This is a musical chairs-style game. When the music begins, participants should pass the balloon to other participants. After the music stops, the person with the balloon should select a piece of paper from a bowl and answer the question to continue the game.


Round 2: Connection

It's a one-on-one game. Five questions are posed to the players. The person who responds first will be declared the winner.


Round 3: Treasure Hunt

There will be hints concealed everywhere. The gamers will receive instructions on how to find the clues. The player must follow those hints in order to find the treasure

MATH PLAY

“Math-Play” and “Fun-O-Fun” this event will have interesting puzzles which includes simple math formulas and idea with 3 rounds followed by strict duration.

MATH PLAY

Event Type: Team (2 Per Team)

Round 1: Fun with Mathematicians

This round will contain a glass bowl full of mathematician’s name and a bat with a ball. Two persons will be allowed to play. The Game is, one person needs to wait near the glass bowl. One person has to hit the ball continuously till they reach their team mate. Once they reached, another person will be allowed to read mathematician’s name from the bowl and vice versa for duration of about 3 minutes.


Round 2: Treasure for a Blind

One person will be blindfolded. Someone else must instruct that blindfolded person to pick the hidden treasure which contains a simple problem. Both must solve and respond within five minutes


Round 3: Blah...Blah...Blah

One person’s mouth will be filled with water who need to face the board. With that, Blah...Blah...Blah...they have to pronounce/dictate a formula by which another person needs to follow their Blah...Blah...Blah and guess it correctly. Once the answer has been found, the Winner and Runner will be announced.

DIVIDE, COMBINE AND CONQUER

This event combines math and fun. Our event is a double delight for the players.

DIVIDE, COMBINE AND CONQUER

Event Type: Group

Round 1: Tricky cups

The coin will be mentioned on the back of the cups. A participant will be given a bowl of coins. The player must pick a coin from a bowl and place it in a cup using a fork . The player who dropped the most coins into the designated cups wins.


Round 2: Puzzle hunt

The player will be given simple math questions to solve, and by doing so, they will receive a three-digit code to unlock the safe box. Inside the box will be an image.


Round 3: Jeopardy board

Players must choose a number from a list of topics. Each number represents a question in that topic; if the player correctly answers the question, they will be rewarded with points. The player with the highest number of points (100, 200, 500) wins.

FUNKY FEST

This event is totally fun, but with a bit of simple math like graphs and shapes mixed in. Participants can thoroughly enjoy their day.

FUNKY FEST

Event Type: Team (4 per team)

Round 1: Whisper Challenge

The purpose of the game is to guess what the other person is saying while wearing headphones and listening to loud music. This is a great team game. One of the participants has to whisper the given statement, while the other has to read his or her lips and find the correct statement.


Round 2: Doodle Challenge

People are using their artistic skills and doodling in their spare time to complete this challenge. But there's a catch. The challenge requires two participants: one must draw on the other's back while the second attempts to recreate the image solely by feeling the movements of the pen.


Round 3: Hack with Math

Math is a hack. The objective of the game is to decrypt the encrypted laptop and find the name of a mathematician displayed on the screen. To decode the password, players must solve the riddles. The password will be numeric, alphabetic, or alphanumeric in nature.

This event combines math and fun. Our event is a double delight for the players.

PAPER PRESENTATION

Event Type: Individual or Team (2per team)

Event Description:

Students should present their ideas about the following topics:

  1. Work of Ramanujan and its Applications
  2. Cryptocurrency and Block chains
  3. Mathematics and Art
  4. Graph Theory in Google Map
  5. Mathematics in social media (except WhatsApp)
  6. Mathematics in Control System
  7. Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics
  8. Computer Graphics
  9. Game theory
  10. Data Structures and Algorithm
  11. Mathematics in Machine Learning
  12. Data science
  13. Data Analysis

The presentation should include short intro, a brief description of the topic and unique conclusion.
