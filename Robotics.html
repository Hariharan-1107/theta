ROBOCRAZE

In this event, the speed, accuracy, efficiency and power skills of the robot will be evaluated.

ROBOCRAZE

Event Type: Team of 4 members

ROUND 1: (Maze Mania)

A maze track will be provided to the participants. In which they have to find the correct path and reach the finish point in less time.


ROUND 2: (Resisting Obstacles)

In this round, the participants have to drive on a new track which is filled with obstacles. Overcoming the obstacles and reaching the finish line in the minimum time leads the participants to the final.


ROUND 3: (Robo Sakka)

Qualified participants are allowed to play 1-1 robot soccer matches. Based on the points scored in rounds 1 & 2 the match fixtures will be made.
