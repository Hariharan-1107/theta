TREASURE HUNT

Description:

  • Each team is presented with a same set of puzzles or clues which the team had to solve in order to find a piece of information.

  • Game consists of 5 rounds of puzzles.
  • Each puzzle leads to the next location (walkable distance) within the campus which is the next round and use a vital piece of information to finish the quest.

JODO JEETHO

Jodo Jeetho consists of 3 rounds.

JODO JEETHO

Event Type: Team (2 Per Team)

ROUND 1: ‘JIL’

Connect the pictures and find out the word within the given time. (It can be anything such as name of a movie or a celebrity...)


ROUND 2: ‘JUNG’

In this round, clues are given one by one that appears in a movie. The corresponding movie names are to be guessed within the given time.


ROUND 3: ‘JUK’

In this round, connect the given clues and find the famous movie dialogue within the given time.

KICK THE LUCK

SENSE OF SCIENCE (SOS)

This consists of three rounds. Each round has different method of playing.

SENSE OF SCIENCE (SOS)

Event Type: Team (2 per team)

ROUND 1: SHOT WITH DART

  • The balloons are stick to the wall; behind the balloons letters are pasted in random order.
  • The participant should pop the balloons using dart. Behind the balloon there must be some letter. The participant should guess some science related word regarding the letter within a given time.
  • Clues are given to the teammate by the player indirectly and they should guess the answer.

ROUND 2: FOLLOW THE LEADS

In 2nd round, behind the cups alphabet letters are given. Clues are shown in projector. They should arrange the cups in correct order regarding the answer.


ROUND 3: WIRE LOOP GAME

In this round, a current conducting wire with twist and turned loops is provided. The participant should guide the metal hoop without touching the wire. If it touches the wire, or you don’t complete it in time, then the buzzer will beep and you will fail.
