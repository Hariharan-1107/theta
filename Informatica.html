Our Events

IDEATHON

Eligibility : Anyone who is interested and is having a tech-based idea to present.

Theme : Only Tech based Project Ideas are welcome.

Rules:

  1. MAXIMUM MEMBERS IN A TEAM IS 2
  2. MAIN OBJECTIVE IS PROJECT SUBMISSION
  3. THEME : ANY TECHNICAL THEME
  4. IDEA SHOULD BE PRESENTED IN PPT WITH PRETEMPLATED FORMAT
  5. NO PLAGIARISM IS ALLOWED

CAPTURE THE FLAG

A Capture the Flag (CTF) event is a cybersecurity competition where participants try to solve a series of challenges that involve hacking, cryptography, forensics, and other skills related to cybersecurity.

Rules:

  • Laptop Must
  • Students will be asked to find flags/bugs in a given application in a given time frame

Requirements:

  • Computer
  • College network

CODE A THON

Software Development Competition that challenges participants to develop a project or solution in a limited amount of time to a given scenario.

The best 3 Solutions will be Shortlisted and provided with prizes.
Theme : Questions/Scenarios will be given along with test cases and students are required to solve the concepts.

Rules:

  1. MAXIMUM MEMBERS IN A TEAM IS 2
  2. ALL THE TEAM MEMBERS MUST KNOW CODING

TECH QUIZ

Join us for an exciting Tech Quiz competition, where you can showcase your knowledge and skills in the world of technology! Our Tech Quiz event is designed to challenge participants with questions and tasks related to various areas of technology, including software development, computer hardware, networking, cybersecurity, and more.

The event is open to individuals and teams of up to four members, with separate tracks for students and professionals. Participants will compete against each other to answer challenging questions and complete tasks that test their understanding of the latest technology trends, tools, and techniques.


Our Tech Quiz event will be hosted in a fun and interactive format, with rounds of questions and tasks that get progressively more difficult. You'll have the chance to show off your skills, compete against other participants, and win exciting prizes!
