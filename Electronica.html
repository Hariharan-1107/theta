Who Am I ?

Event Type : 2 Per Team

Round 1 - Component Quest

We will ask indirect questions and participants have to answer the names of the components.


Round 2 - Device Detective

We will ask indirect questions and participants have to answer the devices.


Round 3 - Colour Code Conundrum

We will give resistors and participants have to find the value of resistors using colour coding.
