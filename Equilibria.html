CLASH OF BRAINS

CLASH OF BRAINS

Event Type: Team of 4 members

Round 1:Kludge your caliber

A common question is displayed to all the teams, the game is to find the answer for the question after completing a fun-filled task!!!The task is to be done for each question. The questions are of the type connections, odd one out, slogans, taglines, logos etc. Teams with top scores are qualified for next round. Hone your skills!!


Round2: Pricing the product

A set of products along with their prices are displayed to the teams for a certain time, the team has to memorize the products and prices. The game is to solve the questions which can be in the form of word problems, simplifications, solving simple equations, logical reasoning etc., which ultimately gives the answer of the prices. Questions all related to already displayed products. The teams who finds the accurate prices by solving the questions within the given time constraints are moved to the next round. Hurry before the stock runs out & enjoy the best rates!!


Round 3: Treasure Hunt

A perfect game to experience the thrill of discovery! Follow the clues and find your fortune!!!The game is about finding the clues by solving them which are placed in different places in the premises. The team which finds the last clue and uncover the treasure first will be the overall winner of this event.

Sparkles

SPARKLES

Event Type: Team participation (2 per team)

Round 1:Quiz master

The participants will be asked with “QUIZ - Questions” from different fields and the teams answering first after completing certain fun tasks will be given marks. Team with maximum scores will be moved to next round


Round 2:Rapid fire round

It's a brand awareness game and one-one game. A product name will be given to the teams. Each team has to bet the maximum number of brands they can quote for that product.Teams accomplishing such count within the given time will be given a point.Teams with maximum points move for the final round


Round 3: Yota Bite

It's a memory game. A picture will posted for few seconds, the participants need to observe it .With the given time, they have to rearrange and arrive at the given shuffled picture

Seeds

SEEDS

Event Type: Team (2 Per Team)

Round 1: Hidden truth

A small portion of a least popular advertisement will be telecasted The participants will be asked to find the company based on the concept of the advertisement. The team leading the score board will be moved to next level


Round 2: Greeny Red

In this round, each team will be given with green and red balls and will be given a topic. Based on the colour they chose, they will be asked to speak in favour of and against the given topic. The team speaking fluently, with no repetition will be awarded.


Round 3: Business plan

Team need to pick a balloon which will be marked as product or services. Based on what they select they need to prepare the business plan within a given time Based on what they select they need to prepare the plan in the given time. The most feasible plans will be awarded.

MYSTERIO

MYSTERIO

Event Type: Team (2 per team)

Round 1: BOW LINE

A picture with multiple products in it will ve shown in projector to one of the participants while the other stays outside the room. After the given time, the other participant with the instruction received from his/her teammate has to write the maximum number of objects he could recollect. Teams writing maximum count will be moved to next round


Round 2: CONNECTION

A series of images will be posted, connecting which will give the name of a company or celebrity, movie or any meaningful word.


Round 3: CROSSWORD PUZZLES

The teams will be given with a printed crossword puzzles. Teams solving the puzzle correctly in the least time will be awarded.

Pivotal

PIVOTAL

Event Type: Team (2 per team)

Round 1: Find Your Brain Fitness

This round will be a combination of finding the jumbled words, framing new words from the given single word, puzzles etc., in the given time

Round 2: Fantastical Creation

The teams have to create posters for the product allocated to them. Posters with attractive look and meaningful theme will be selected for the next round

Round 3: Vigilance Checking
