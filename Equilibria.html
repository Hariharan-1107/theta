CLASH OF BRAINS

CLASH OF BRAINS

Event Type: Team of 4 members

Round 1: Know Your Company (KYC)

The team need to find the names of the company based on the clues provided along with completing a fun task. It may be of finding the company name by company’s logo, tagline, finding the duplicate logo amongst the real ones.


Round2: Pricing the product

A set of products and their prices will be displayed for a minute. The task will be to solve the set of questions like equations, word problems, logical reasoning which may consist the price for the product shown. The team solving the equations and figuring out the accurate price as shown will be awarded


Round 3: Treasure Hunt

Clues will be placed in different locations, by tracing the clues, the participants have to chase for the ultimate prize/treasure

SEEDS

SEEDS

Event Type: Team (2 Per Team)

Round 1: Quiz Masters

The participants will be posted with quizzes and the teams answering first and based on the marks scored at the end, teams will be shortlisted for the next round.


Round 2: Rapid fire round

For the teams selected, rapid fire round on the theme of brand awareness will be conducted and each team will be betting the maximum number of answers they can provide and attempt fulfilling the betting, teams successfully accomplishing their targets will be give scores and moved forward to next round


Round 3: Yota bite

The participants will be posted a picture and given a time of minute time during which they need to observe that, then they’ll be asked to rearrange the given shuffled puzzle based on what they observe in the picture.

SPARKLES

SPARKLES

Event Type: Team participation (2 per team)

Round 1: Hidden truth

A least popular advertisement will be telecasted and the participants will be asked to find the company based on the concept of the advertisement. The team leading the score board will be moved to next level


Round 2: Greeny Red

In this round, each team will be given with green and red balls and will be given a topic. Based on the colour of the ball they choose they need to speak in favour and against the topic


Round 3: Business plan

The final round is to prepare a business plan either for a product or service. The team need to pick up a balloon which will be marked as product or service. Based on what they select they need to prepare the plan in the given time. The most feasible plans will be awarded.

MYSTERIO

MYSTERIO

Event Type: Team (2 per team)

Round 1: BOW LINE

The event checks the memory power of the teams participating. Number of products will be displayed and they need to memorize the products and recall the maximum number of products they remember


Round 2: CONNECTION

A collection of pictures will be posted and the team needs to find a word or theme based on the connection between the pictures


Round 3: CROSSWORD PUZZLES

The selected teams will be given some crossword puzzles and teams solving the puzzles in less time will be awarded.

PIVOTAL

PIVOTAL

Event Type: Team (2 per team)

Round 1: Find Your Brain Fitness

This round will be a combination of Jumbled Words, Framing the Word, Cross word.

Round 2: Fantastical Creation

Participants shortlisted from round 1, need to create a product poster design and they need to market it. Attractive posters will be selected.

Round 3: Vigilance Checking

The round is to evaluate how participants handle the situation by overcoming the distractions imposed on them.
