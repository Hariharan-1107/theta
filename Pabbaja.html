Our Events

KALAM

This event is to bring out the speaking skills and analyze thought process of the participants with entertaining game events.

KALAM

Round 1: “OBJECTION YOUR HONOUR”

Participants will be given a random topic and will be asked to speak about the topic for 3 mins and the same person will speak on different perspective of the same topic after 3 mins. Winner will be selected on the basis of their thought process.


Round 2: “MY KARUTHU WHAT IS I’M SAYING..”

Participants will be allowed to choose a topic from the given list. 2 minutes will be allocated for the speaker and at the end of 2nd min, the speaker will select a member from another team to share their opinions. Participants will be selected based on their voice tone, speech style

ALL IN ALL AZHAGURAJA

This is a 100% fun event with exciting games and tasks. The team which finishes the game series with minimum time and has maximum points are selected as winners.

ALL IN ALL AZHAGURAJA

Refreshment Round: ‘Simon Says!’

  • Round 1:

    Game 1:“MUDINJA KANDUPUDI”: All the players in the team pull a cup of water using a tissue roll and fill another empty cup using straw. First player who finishes this task, is allowed to touch a given object/toy and gives 5 clues to the other players. And those players are supposed find the object using the clues.


    Game 2:“UNNA KEKALA DA.....”: In this game the participants play a pictionary game simultaneously with ‘The lawyer’ game..


    Game 3:“SONAMUTHA POCHA?...”: In this game one of the team player draws an object using instructions given by their team. The picture to be drawn is selected by playing a game with another team.


    Bonus Round: Match the number! Catch the bonus!!


    Game 4:“AAH..THALLU THALLU THALLU THALLU....!”: The players should answer the previous question asked by the event organizers while not letting the balloon to fall. The players cannot touch the balloon but can just blow it to prevent it from falling..


    Game 5:“EPPA PAATHALUM SAAPADU SAAPADU”: Selected teams answer a common question. One of the members ties a blind fold & eats a candy which is hung. The team can answer the question only after the person with blind fold finishes eating the candy.

  • Round 2(auditorium)

    4 teams are selected from round 1

    Game 1:”CINEMA! CINEMA!! ” : Selected teams from Round 1 guess the movie name by connecting the pictures. (Connection game).


    Game 2:“ENGLISH THERIYADHA?”: Each team translates the given Tamil movie scene to English.


    Game 3:”PAATU PAADAVA?”: Each team are asked to complete the song lyrics or guess the song by its karaoke.

THAMIZHODU VILAIYAADU

Thamizhodu Vilaiyaadu is a fun interactive game series among team members, integrated with Tamil literature. (3 per team)

THAMIZHODU VILAIYAADU
  • Round 1:

    Stage 1:MANDA MELA IRUKURA KONDAIYA PAARU: This is a super fun game where the participants will find a word written in a cap on their head using the clues given by their team.


    Stage 2:KURAL SOLLAVAA?: In this game the team have to find the Thirukural said by their team member with water in their mouth.


    Stage 3:“I’M WAITING”: The team has to wait for the question until their team member completes a series of tasks given. The team which finds the answer for maximum no. of questions will be qualified to next round.

  • Round 2:

    Stage 1:YAARADHU.... YAARADHU?: The team is supposed to find the Tamil poet or novelist by their sayings or poems. Participants will be asked blow the ball to the other end of the cup before actually presenting their answers.


    Stage 2:EDHEI....KEKKALA......SATHAMA.....!!: Event organizers will read a tongue twister once, to one person of the team. This has to be passed on to the successive members secretly. If a person wants to hear the tongue twister again he/she will be asked to perform a task to hear the twister again. So, Sharpen your ears!!!!.


    Stage 3:“PONNINADHI PAAKANUME”: A character name from Ponniyin Selvan is jumbled in a box. One of the team player with blind fold arranges the letters. The character has to be found by the other teammates using the clues given by the organizers and they help their member in arranging the letters of the character.

RAJAPAT

Rajapat brings out the star actor in you. So, Lights! Camera! Rolling! Action!

RAJAPAT

  • Round 1:

    DEI...PARAMA! NADI DA!!!!! A concept or scene is given which the participants are asked to act in their style. They are selected based on their acting skills and creative approach to the given concept or scene.

  • Round 2:

    ADANGAPPA IDHU ULAGA NADIPU DA SAAMY!! A story line is given and the qualified people are asked to develop the story by acting for a specified time.

“PENAMUNAIYIL PESU”

This event focuses on creative thinking and writing skills of participants amidst of exciting tasks

PIVOTAL

  • Round 1:

    “ORU KADHA SOLLATA SIR?” A common topic or a concept is given to the participants and they are asked to write a story of their own within the stipulated time.

  • Round 2:

    “ORU OORLA....” In this round, the selected people from the previous round is given a story line and are asked to develop the story. The challenge is that they are supposed to include various emotions given by the event organizers in their story.
