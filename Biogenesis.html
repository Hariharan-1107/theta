Our Events

THE BIG BANG THEORY

The much-awaited funny event is aimed at testing the participant’s thinking skills with fun activities. Participants need to think and act quickly. Let the speedster in you, get the rewards.

THE BIG BANG THEORY

Event Type: Team (Two per team)

Round 1: PICTURE THIS!

Each team will be given a set of scientific words which they will have to explain without words by drawing on the board (Pictionary). For the correct answer points will be allotted. The top 15 teams will be shortlisted for the next round.


Round 2: ACTION REPLAY...

In this round, one of the members from each qualifying team will receive questions related to science which they will have to explain by acting them out without the usage of words (Charades). The second member of each team will have to guess the answer from the acted- out hints. The teams getting the most correct answers within the given time will move on to the next round.


Round 3: FINDERS KEEPERS!!!

It is an exciting scavenger hunt game. Participants have to find a symbol/sign/object by following a series of hints. The hints can be obtained by completing intermediate tasks. The prize is waiting for you so get ready to run!

BRAIN CHASERS

This event is aimed at testing the participant’s creative thinking and strategies through funny games. Be fast and get rewards.

BRAIN CHASERS

Event Type: Team (Two per team)

Round 1: HAWKEYE!

Two images will be given side by side. Those images will have certain differences. Participants will have to spot and identify the differences in those images and get a verification stamp from the referee which they will only get after winning a random game against them.


Round 2: SHERLOCK HOLMES...

Clues and evidence of a particular crime will be provided. The participants will have to solve each clue and connect the pieces of evidence to find out the criminal. Let us bring out your inner detective!


HUMANS & LADDERS

25 grids will be drawn on the floor and one member from each team will be the player. Each grid will have a riddle which is to be answered by the other member and he/she will be given a chance to roll the dice if the answer is correct. One who reaches the 25th grid first will be the winner.

SMARTICLES

The most popular event i.e., the quiz tournament aims to test the thinking skills as well as the scientific knowledge of the participants with a surprise twist.

SMARTICLES

Event Type: Team (Two per team)

Round 1: DYK?

This round will consist of general knowledge plus science-based quiz. Maximum points will be awarded if the participant gets the answer on the first try. If they answer wrong, the question will be passed on to the next team and they will get a chance to partially steal the points.


Round 2: BREATHE OUT...

This round will be a special round with photo-based questions focusing on scientists, scientific inventions, and other science-related content. A chance for the participants to relax and enjoy before entering the next round.


Round 3: BLINK & YOU’LL MISS IT!

The last round will be a lightning round with general science-based questions. The focus will be on getting as many correct answers as possible within the given time. The team with the most correct answers is the winner..

MODEL EXHIBIT

This event aims to develop a scientific spirit and curiosity among participants which in turn forces them to think and creatively find solutions to the challenges.

MODEL EXHIBIT

Event Type: Teams/Individual

Round:

This event consists of a single round in which the participants need to explain their model briefly (usually not more than 5 minutes) to the judges.


Rules:

  • Participating team will have to bear all expenses for doing their model.
  • Models that do not fit the title will not be taken into consideration.
  • Participating teams will have to make their own boarding arrangements at the venue of the exhibition.
  • The exhibit may be either a working/non-working model.
  • Any number of participants can form a team but prize will be given on model basis i.e., one model can only win one prize regardless of number of participants in that team.

Topics:

  • Recent Trends in Disease Therapy
  • Photosynthesis & Plant Hormones
  • 3D Printing
  • Public Health Hazard Management
  • Quorum Sensing & Cell Signalling
  • Biosensors

MEMORIES BRING BACK YOU...

This is an event designed especially for final year students. It’ll give the soon-to-be departing students a chance to prepare and design their own memorabilia which will allow them to remember and bring back their college days after they’ve graduated.

MEMORIES BRING BACK YOU

Event Type: Any final year participant

Part 1: PALETTE

In this portion, the participants will be given the freedom to choose and artistically design/portray their memories on a canvas. Supervision & guidance will be available if needed on the site.


Part 2: REELS

In this event, participants will have to submit a short video compilation (2-3 mins) of their cherished college days and memories. The top 3 best video submissions will get the prize.


Part 3: WALL OF FAME

A photo wall filled with the memories of final year students of various departments will be set up and anyone can come, reminisce and take pictures with it.

Note:

  • The participants will have to pay for their respective canvasses and paints (will be provided on site).
  • Video submission should have a fraction of on-site footage rather than consisting entirely of animations.
  • This event is only limited to final year/graduating students.
