Our Events

RRR

A stage to bring out the participants' spontaneity and multi-potentiality in a fun filled way. We are going to conduct 3 rounds, and the participants will be selected based on the points that they have earned within the given time.

RRR

TYPE: Team (2 per team) EVENT

FIRST ROUND: REFLECT

The first phase of the event is REFLECT. Where the participants will be selecting a famous personality, every 30 seconds the participants will be given different reactions using a spin wheel and they are expected to correlate the emotions with the personality and make a spontaneous transition between the feelings for 2 minutes.


SECOND ROUND: REACT

The second phase is REACT. A muted video of the selected personality will be played and the participants must rightly guess the context and content of the conversation or they can cook it up in a funny way.


FINAL ROUND: RESTATE

The third phase is RESTATE. One of the participants will prepare a speech content in English and deliver the same in the style of personality chosen. The other will have to translate the content into Tamil on spot.

PS II

An amazing opportunity to express their knowledge on proverbs and as well as their artistic skill through a poster sketch.

PS II

FIRST ROUND: FILL THE PROVERBS

A balloon with incomplete proverb or Thirukural will be circulated among the participants, the participant who holds the balloon at the tilt of the music should complete the proverb or Thirukural. Only the one who complete will be selected for the next level.


SECOND ROUND: BLIND SEARCH

The second round is an Advantage round to choose your topics for the poster. In this task the letters from A to Z will be shuffled and pasted on a wall. Out of 2participants one will be blindfolded, while the other participant will give directions to select the needed letters for the name of the topic they have chosen. If the participants fail to take the letters needed within 1 minute, then they have to present their posters for the topic given on the spot.


THIRD ROUND: POSTER PRESENTATION

In the third round ; based on the topic chosen in the advantage round, the participants must sketch and present the poster as a team. The time allotted for making the poster is 1 hour and has to present for minimum of 4 minutes

SING IN THE RAIN

SING IN THE RAIN' is an art of re-orchestration that can be done through alively fishing bowl where the participants should show their witty lyrical talent with an hour of time.

SING IN THE RAIN

There will be two fishing bowls, one contains songs and the other has titles of different sectors. The participants will have to select one from each bowl and remix the lyrics of song according to the topic. An hour will be given within which the participants will have to remix and sing their song.
