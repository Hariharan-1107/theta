RRR (Reflect , React , Restate)

Last Date for Registration :
- 04 - 2023

Access

EVENT TYPE: Team (2 per team) EVENT

DESCRIPTION:

A stage to bring out the participants' spontaneity and multi-potentiality in a fun filled way. We are going to conduct 3 rounds, and the participants will be selected based on the points that they have earned within the given time.

FIRST ROUND: REFLECT

first phase of the event is REFLECT. Where the participants will be selecting a famous personality, every 30 seconds the participants will be given different reactions using a spin wheel and they are expected to correlate the emotions with the personality and make a spontaneous transition between the feelings for 2 minutes.

SECOND ROUND: REACT

second phase is REACT. A muted video of the selected personality will be played and the participants must rightly guess the context and content of the conversation or they can cook it up in a funny way.

FINAL ROUND: RESTATE

third phase is RESTATE. One of the participants will prepare a speech content in English and deliver the same in the style of personality chosen. The other will have to translate the content into Tamil on spot.
